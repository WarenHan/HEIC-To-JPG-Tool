========================================================================
                      HEIC TO JPG CONVERTER v2
                    Portable Utility User Guide
========================================================================

Welcome to your portable, zero-setup photo utility suite. Because these 
tools are standalone, you can run them from any folder on your computer 
(Desktop, Documents, USB Flash Drive, etc.) without requiring administrator 
permissions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. THE FILES & FOLDERS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Both executables automatically check the directory they are currently 
sitting in. The first time you run the tool, it will automatically build
the workspace folders right next to it:

Your Workspace Folder/
│
├── heic_to_jpg-V2.exe    <-- The main photo converter tool
├── clean_folders-V2.exe  <-- The folder maintenance utility
│
├── input/                <-- Place your raw HEIC photos here
└── output/               <-- Grab your finished JPEGs here

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. HOW TO CONVERT PHOTOS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open the "input" folder and paste your raw .heic or .HEIC images.
2. Double-click "heic_to_jpg-V2.exe".
3. The console window will display how many photos were discovered.
4. Press [ENTER] to confirm and begin the conversion process.
5. Watch the real-time progress. Once completed, your fresh, 
   high-quality JPEGs will be waiting in the "output" folder.

* EMERGENCY STOP: You can press [Ctrl + C] or close the window at any
  time to halt the conversion instantly.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. CLEANING UP & MAINTENANCE
------------------------------------------------------------------------
To give you complete data security, the converter never automatically 
wipes your files. When you are 100% confident in your new JPEGs and want
to tidy up your workspace:

1. Double-click "clean_folders-V2.exe".
2. The tool will check both folders and ask you independently:
   -> "Do you want to delete all photos inside Input? (y/n):"
   -> "Do you want to delete all photos inside Output? (y/n):"
3. Type 'y' and hit Enter to completely empty a folder, or 'n' to safely
   leave it exactly as it is.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SYSTEM RELIABILITY NOTICE:
Both tools run completely locally on your hardware. No images or data 
are ever transmitted over the network or internet.
